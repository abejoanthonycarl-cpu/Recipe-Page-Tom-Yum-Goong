Tom Yum Goong ต้มยำกุ้ง

Tom yum soup with shrimp is the most popular kind of tom yum in Thailand. Including shrimp heads adds extra flavour to the soup.

Preparation time

  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 20-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Medium to large shrimp (preferably head-on, shell-on)
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Fish sauce
  • Thai chilies (like Bird’s Eye Chilli)
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes (Roma tomatoes are often used)
  • Sweet white onions
  • Lime juice
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil water or chicken broth:in a pot and add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. Let it simmer for 5-10 minutes to infuse the flavors..
  2. Add mushrooms, sliced onion, tomatoes, and chopped galangal:to the broth and cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables soften.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until they turn pink and opaque:about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Stir in fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar: (if using) to taste and adjust the seasoning.
  5. Remove from heat: add fresh coriander leaves on top before serving.For a creamier version, you can add evaporated milk or coconut milk at the last stage. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve hot. Enjoy your Tom Yum Goong, a flavorful and aromatic Thai classic
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
